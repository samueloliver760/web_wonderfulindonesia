All about Indonesia

Profile

Indonesia or the official name is Republic of Indonesia is a country located in Asia, specifically in South East Asia. Indonesia's capital city is Jakarta. With the population of 274,8 million peoples, Indonesia is the 4th largest population countries in the world behind China, United States and Russia. Indonesia has over 17.000 island that's why Indonesia has the nickname "The maritim countries". The official language in Indonesia is Indonesian. Furthermore, the majority of Indonesian people is Muslim (86,7 % of Indonesian population). Indonesia is lead by President named Joko Widodo who already became the president since 2014 (Win the president election twice 2014 and 2019) and helped by vice president named Ma'ruf Amin.

Time Zone

Indonesia has 3 time zones which is divided like following:

  1. WIB (Western Indonesian Time)

    2. WIB is used by Western part of Indonesia such as all of Sumatra and Java Islands, West and Central Kalimantan. This time zone is 7 hours slower from GMT.


  2. WITA (Central Indonesian Time)

    3. WITA is used by Central part of Indonesia such as South, East and North Kalimantan, all of Sulawesi and Bali island, East and West Nusa Tenggara. This times zone is one hour slower than WIB and 8 hours slower from GMT.


  3. WIT (East Indonesian Time)

    4. WIT is used by Eastern part of Indonesia such as Maluku and Papua. This times zone is two hour slower than WIB, one hour slower than WITA and 9 hour slower than GMT

Currency

The currency of Indonesia is Rupiah. The currency code is IDR with the symbol is Rp. Rupiah is widely used by local and accepted almost all shop, hotel and restaurants across the nation. Indonesian money has 2 types which are banknotes and coin. The backnotes nominal are from 1000 , 2000, 5000, 10000, 20000, 50000 to 100000 rupiah while the coin nominal are from 50, 100, 200, 500 to 1000 rupiah. Indonesia banknotes is decorated with nation heroes such as former president and vice president, Soekarno and Moh Hatta, Sam Ratulangi, Juanda and etc.
